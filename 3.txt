What are the benefits of using meta tags.
Solutuon:-

i) Improved Organization:
- It can help business to organize digital assets by adding descriptive information such as keywords, titles, and descriptions.

ii) Consistency:
- A metadata tagging system can help to ensure that all digital assets are consistent with brand guidelines by adding standardized tags and descriptions.

iii) Time-Saving:
- Metadata tagging can save time by automating the process of adding descriptive information to digital assets.

iv) Relevancy:
- Meta tags show search engines how relevant your page is to the user's query.

v) They optimize our site without making things untidy:
- Meta tag do not affect the actual Visible content of our websites in any way.
